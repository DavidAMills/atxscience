Protecting the Air We Breath

ATX Science is a proponent for the scientific education and encouragement of individual involvement and progress. ATX Science works to advance the local, state, and federal education system as well as the science regarding scientific innovation. ATX Science’s work helps to advance technological infrastructure, which helps to increase overall community outcomes. Our programs include sending out ambassadors to raise social consciousness about the cause on a local and global level, and to hold fundraising events in order to provide assistance to those communities with heightened risks due to industry, transportation, or other circumstances.

Vision

To Innovate and to Inspire Innovation

ATX Science aims to positively affect communities at local, state, federal, and international scales. Extenuating circumstances impact our lives at all scales, and there is ample room for improvement at all levels. We aspire to utilize technology to bridge scientific gaps and transcend socio-economic circumstances. We are committed to the task of advocating for accessible and robust technology solutions until all people are afforded the same quality solutions.

Partnership

Collaborating to Improve Outcomes

We aim to partner with state, local and tribal communities in identifying and profiling air pollution sources, assessing and applying emerging measurement methods, characterizing the degree and extent of local air pollution problems, providing information for mitigating personal exposure to air pollution, and advocating for the reduction of air polluting activities.

Sustainability

Passion for Ecology Meets Technology

Many air quality technologies deployed today are high-cost, high-upkeep, and immobile. Technology has improved at a pace that has far exceeded widespread implementation. Some cities nearing 1,000,000 population have 6 or less air quality sensors across their Geographic area. ATX Science leverages the latest technologies which drive down costs while increasing data gathering capability at spatial and temporal scales. We can do this while maintaining or more often improving accuracy and efficiency of gathering and analyzing data.

Citizen Science

Empowerment Through Data

As professional open-source networks such as Github and Research Gate have emerged, we often find technology innovation is readily available to those who are willing to learn. We want to teach those who are willing to learn how they can use the latest technology to advocate for their local ecology. Knowledge is power and giving citizens the means to gather data is empowerment.

Research

Our team consists of proven and published researchers who are experts in their fields. We are passionate about analyzing data and leveraging resources with evidence-based approaches. Using a focused geographic perspective we seek to understand each environment and those within it from the relevant context of the individuals within the community.
