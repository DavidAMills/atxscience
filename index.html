Education

We can't fix a problem if we don't know that it is there. One of the first steps to improving our environment is to tell people about it! We want to help you educate citizens, students, and professors on how they can use technologies to learn about and promote awareness about their local ecology.

Advocacy

Are you working to improve the environment in your local area? Have you received a grant, or are you in the process of writing an environmentally focused grant? Let us bring our expertise of science, technology, and evidence-based methodologies to your project!

Smart Networks

AtmoVision uses leading-edge technologies to develop smart networks. We can build customized sensors to measure pollutants for any air quality related project. A custom built sensor can deliver real-time data by utilizing existing wireless infrastructure. Or, we can deploy a very low-cost and reliable wireless network that maximizes the data gathering potential of your geographic area!

Mission

Protecting the Air We Breath

AtmoVision is a proponent for the scientific education and encouragement of individual involvement and activism regarding ecology. AtmoVision works to advance the local, state, and federal education system as well as the science regarding air quality. AtmoVision’s work helps to preserve clean air, which helps to increase overall community health. Our programs include sending out ambassadors to raise social consciousness about the cause on a local and global level, and to hold fundraising events in order to provide assistance to those communities with heightened pollution risks due to industry, transportation, or other human-induced pollution causing activities.

Vision

To Innovate and to Inspire Innovation

Atmovision aims to positively affect communities at local, state, federal, and international scales. Air quality impacts our lives at all scales, and there is ample room for improvement at all levels. We aspire to utilize technology to bridge scientific gaps and transcend socio-economic circumstances. Clean breathable air is an inalienable right and we are committed to the task of advocating for clean air until all air-breathing life has clean and breathable air.

Partnership

Collaborating to Improve Outcomes

We aim to partner with state, local and tribal communities in identifying and profiling air pollution sources, assessing and applying emerging measurement methods, characterizing the degree and extent of local air pollution problems, providing information for mitigating personal exposure to air pollution, and advocating for the reduction of air polluting activities.

Sustainability

Passion for Ecology Meets Technology

Many air quality technologies deployed today are high-cost, high-upkeep, and immobile. Technology has improved at a pace that has far exceeded widespread implementation. Some cities nearing 1,000,000 population have 6 or less air quality sensors across their Geographic area. AtmoVision leverages the latest technologies which drive down costs while increasing data gathering capability at spatial and temporal scales. We can do this while maintaining or more often improving accuracy and efficiency of gathering and analyzing data.

Citizen Science

Empowerment Through Data

As professional open-source networks such as Github and Research Gate have emerged, we often find technology innovation is readily available to those who are willing to learn. We want to teach those who are willing to learn how they can use the latest technology to advocate for their local ecology. Knowledge is power and giving citizens the means to gather data is empowerment.

Research

Relevant Context and Objective Results

Our team consists of proven and published researchers who are experts in their fields. We are passionate about analyzing data and leveraging resources with evidence-based approaches. Using a focused geographic perspective we seek to understand each environment and those within it from the relevant context of the individuals within the community. We want to meet people where they are.
